Fun Facts
  • The sun contains 99.8% of all the mass in the solar system.
  • Even though the sun is huge compared to the planets, it's relatively small compared to other stars in the universe.
  • The sun is comprised of 92.1% hydrogen and 7.8% helium.
  • The sun is 93 million miles away from Earth.
