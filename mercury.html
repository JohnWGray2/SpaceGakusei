Fun Facts
  • Mercury is the smallest planet in the solar system.
  • Despite being the closest to the Sun, Mercury is the second hottest planet in the solar system.
  • Mercury has an atmosphere, and it's the thinnest of all the planets.
  • As of 2020, there have only been 2 missions to Mercury; the Mariner 10 and MESSENGER missions.
